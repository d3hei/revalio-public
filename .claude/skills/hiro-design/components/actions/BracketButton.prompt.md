Hiro's signature `[ LABEL ]` monospace control — used for newsletter CTAs and marketing chrome. Wraps its label in literal brackets; hover flips to a solid fill.

```jsx
<BracketButton filled>Subscribe</BracketButton>
<BracketButton>Read the guide</BracketButton>
```

`filled` for the ink/violet solid style, outline otherwise. Sizes `sm | md | lg`.
